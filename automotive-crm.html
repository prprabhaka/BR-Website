What our Platform Delivers

BeetleRim has helped automotive clients integrate fragmented systems, automate dealer operations, unlock real-time insights, and modernize legacy IT—driving faster sales cycles, lower costs, and stronger customer loyalty.

1

Unified Dealer & Customer Management Systems

Integrated CRM and DMS platforms to connect OEMs, dealers, and agencies - enabling real-time access to customer, vehicle, and sales data across the entire network

2

End-to-End Automation of Sales & Service Workflows

Digitized lead handling, test-drive scheduling, aftersales servicing, and warranty management - reducing manual tasks and improving dealer response times.

3

Real-Time Analytics & Performance Dashboards

Deployed data platforms and BI dashboards that give executives visibility into dealer performance, campaign ROI, and customer engagement in real time

Cloud-Based CRM & Mobility Solutions

Migrated legacy systems to scalable, cloud-enabled CRMs with mobile access for sales, service, and parts teams to collaborate instantly across locations.
