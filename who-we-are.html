Who We Are

BeetleRim is a multinational IT consulting firm focused on transforming our clients' vision into actionable business intelligence through custom solutions, data engineering, and AI.

Our services range from strategy development to technology advisory and implementation.

Our Expertise

From strategy and technology advisory to implementation and operations, BeetleRim delivers tailored solutions that drive growth and efficiency.

We enable them to modernize, automate, and scale through our proven frameworks and global delivery capabilities.

Our human-centered approach, combined with decades of technical expertise, ensures communication and quality.

With a team of industry experts, technology advisors, and functional consultants spread across multiple geographies, BeetleRim offers onsite, offshore, and hybrid delivery models that align with client needs and cost efficiencies. This flexibility allows us to deliver world-class quality and speed without compromise.
